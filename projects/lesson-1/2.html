All About Google

What is Google?

Google is the world's most popular search engine, headquartered in Mountain View, California. It also owns a number of other products, including Android, Google Drive, and YouTube.
Their logo can be seen at https://www.google.com/images/branding/googlelogo/2x/googlelogo_color_272x92dp.png

Who competes with Google?

Google's main competitor in the search engine market is Microsoft's Bing. Google, however, has maintained a large lead in market share that Bing has not been able to challenge, as can be seen by this graph: http://www.conductor.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/05/Screenshot-2014-05-27-16.27.46-1024x589.png

How big is Google?

Google is a publicly traded company (NASDAQ: GOOG) with a market cap of $435 and around 56,000 employees.
