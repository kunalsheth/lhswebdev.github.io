2016 Democratic Party Candidates

There are only a few legitimate 2016 Democratic Party candidates, let's check them out!

Hillary Clinton

The wife of Bill Clinton, Hillary has also served as Secretary of State under President Obama as well as a New York State Senator. Clinton is currently winning in the majority of national primary polls, and is seen as an establishment favorite on the Democratic side.

Bernie Sanders

A self-declared democratic socialist, Bernie is the longest-serving independent in congressional history. He has managed to grow a large following of crowds who welcome him wherever he speaks. Sanders is currently seen as a relative outsider to the Democratic party who poses the largest threat to Clinton's campaign.

Martin O'Malley

Former mayor of Baltimore, Maryland, O'Malley announced his candidacy on May 30, 2015. He advocates raising the minimum wage to $15, re-enacting Glass-Steagall, and creating a national assault weapons ban.
